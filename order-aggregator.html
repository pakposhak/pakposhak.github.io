📦 Per-Brand Order Aggregator

Paste the ITEMS lines from one or many confirmed orders. They'll be grouped by brand, with duplicate products clubbed and quantities summed — ready to send to each brand / wholesaler.
"Load confirmed orders" pulls every order whose status you've set to Payment Confirmed and that hasn't been placed yet — these are the ones ready to order at the brands. Or paste the ITEMS block from any order email — multiple orders are clubbed automatically.
🔑 Account routing — which Gmail orders which brand (optional)
One line per brand, e.g. Khaadi = G1 or Sapphire = shop2@gmail.com. Saved on this device — each brand packet below then shows which account to use.
