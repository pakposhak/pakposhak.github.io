Frequently asked questions

Is this genuine? How do I know I'll receive my order?
Yes. We buy every item directly from the brand's official website, and you get an Order ID to track your order at every step. We only message you on WhatsApp if a product is unavailable.
How is the price calculated?
Your total is the product price, plus shipping (charged by the parcel's weight), plus a small transaction fee. The total you see is the price you pay.
How long does delivery take?
Usually 2–3 weeks from the day we confirm your order, delivered to your address in Bangladesh.
How do I pay?
You can pay by bKash, Nagad, Upay, Rocket or bank transfer, using the payment details shown right after you place your order.
What if my item goes out of stock?
Pakistani fashion sells fast. If anything sells out before we place your order, we tell you immediately on WhatsApp and offer a similar alternative or a full refund.
What if it doesn't fit or arrives damaged?
If your item arrives damaged or doesn't fit, contact us with a photo and we'll do our best to make it right.
