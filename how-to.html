How to order — step by step

A detailed guide for every part of PakiPoshak. It only takes a couple of minutes.

▶ Watch the 1-minute video guide

✨Two easy ways to find what you want

A
Browse products — on the home page tap “🔎 Browse products”, filter by price, category or brand, then tap a product to add it. No copy-paste needed.
B
Browse brands — tap “🏪 Browse brands”. Find a brand by Store Types (multi-department, women’s, premium, menswear, kids) or by Product Category (see which brands make bridal, lawn, winter, etc.), then open the brand and copy a product link (steps below).
💡Not every product is inside “Browse products” yet — for the full range, use “Browse brands”. And if an item is sold out, the app will tell you when you try to save it.

1Add the products you want

1
On the home page, tap a brand (or “Browse all brands”) to open its panel.
2
Tap “Open [Brand] →” — the brand’s own website opens. Find and open the product you want.
3
Tap the ⋮ menu (top-right) and choose “Open in Chrome” (or your phone’s browser).On a computer the brand site opens in a new Chrome tab automatically — just open the product you want.
4
In Chrome, press and hold the web address at the top until “Copy” appears, then tap Copy.Click the web address at the top (or press Ctrl + L), then Ctrl + C to copy it.
5
Come back to PakiPoshak and tap the gold “📋 Paste link” button — the price and sizes fill in automatically.
6
Pick your size(s) and quantity, choose the category if asked, then tap “Save to Order List”.
7
Add another product the same way, or tap “Add Another Product” / continue to your details.
💡Your saved items stay safe even if you close the app or switch pages — they’re kept on your phone until you submit the order.

2Enter your details

1
Type your full name, WhatsApp number, and delivery address in Bangladesh.
2
Tap “Review my order →”.

3Review & pay

1
Check your items and the total (product price + shipping by weight + a small fee).
2
Tap “Confirm & Submit Order” — you’ll get your Order ID.
3
Pay the total using bKash, Nagad, Rocket, Upay or bank — the details are shown right on screen.
4
Under “Already Paid?”, enter the amount you paid, the method, and your TrxID (or attach the receipt screenshot), then tap “Confirm Payment”.
🔒You only pay after you’ve seen and approved the total. The amount you enter is matched to your order automatically.

4Track your order

1
Save your Order ID. Tap “Track My Order” anytime to see its status.
2
We’ll only message you on WhatsApp if a product is unavailable, or the parcel weight turns out much higher than estimated.
